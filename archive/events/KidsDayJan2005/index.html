Bloomington Amateur Radio Club Kid's Day January 2005

Bloomington Amateur Radio Club and Wonderlab held Kid's Day on January 2nd. We had about 12 to 15 kids using the radio, with most of them making a successful contact.
As in June, we held a crystal set building activity as well. We got Elenco crystal set kits from Ramsey Electronics, and had approximately fifteen kids who all built the set and were able to hear the local AM Radio station.

Bloomington Amateur Radio Club gratefully acknowledges Wonderlab's continued support.
