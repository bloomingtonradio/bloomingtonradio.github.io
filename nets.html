Monday Night BARC Nets

Monroe County ARES Net

The Monroe County Amateur Radio Emergency Services (ARES) net is held each Monday night at 7:30 PM on the 146.64 repeater (-136.5 tone) in Bloomington. It is our primary net to practice emergency communications.

Note: If you are a Net Control Operator (NCO), please consult the NCO Schedule to see when you are scheduled to host the net.

South Central Indiana Simplex Net

Immediately following the ARES net at 8:00PM on Mondays there is a Simplex net at 8:00PM at 146.580 (this is our primary Simplex frequency in Monroe County).


Thursday Night BARC Nets

Regular Net

Held each Thursday night at 8:00 PM on the 146.64 repeater (-136.5 tone) in Bloomington. It also has a comments section.

Other Nets
The Indiana Repeater Council keeps a list of coordinated repeaters covering Indiana.

For a comprehensive list of VHF/HF nets, visit WB9FHP's VHF & HF net listings.
