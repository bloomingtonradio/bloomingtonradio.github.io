BARC often works collaboratively with other clubs that operate in and near Bloomington. These include:

  • IU Amateur Radio Club K9IU
  • Bloomington High School South Amateur Radio Club K9SOU
  • Owen County Amateur Radio Association (OCARA), Spencer
  • Hoosier Hills Ham Club, Bedford

Many BARC members are also members of these clubs and vice versa.
