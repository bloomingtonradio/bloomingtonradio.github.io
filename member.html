Joining BARC is easy. Simply come attend a monthly club meeting and talk to a club officer. Or, if you prefer, print out this Membership Application Form (PDF) and mail it in with your dues (described below) to:

Bloomington Amateur Radio Club
P.O. Box 8361
Bloomington, IN 47407-8361

Everyone is welcome to join. All you need is an interest in ham radio for enjoyment, emergency management, skywarn, or to help local events as bike races, etc.

Dues 

Dues are $10/yr for students, $20/yr for individuals, and $35/yr for families, payable each January to Bloomington Amateur Radio Club.

Renewals

Existing members can renew their memberships at a monthly club meeting or by making arrangements with a club officer.
